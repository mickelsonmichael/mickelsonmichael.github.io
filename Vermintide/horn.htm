Tome 1

  • If entering the area from the left gate into the city streets, find an alley on the right leading to the back area and head to the left until you reach a dead end. If entering from the right gate, proceed forward until you come to the dead end.

  • Behind a bush in the corner there will be a cellar. Enter it and turn right.

  • Behind the wall of vines will be a secret entrace with Tome 1 inside.

Grimoire 1

If you are still in the cellar after finding Tome 1, skip to step 3.

  • If entering from the left gate into the city streets, find an alley on the right leading to the back area and head to the left until you reach a dead end. If entering from the right gate, proceed forward until you come to the dead end.

  • Behind a bush in the corner there will be a cellar.

  • Above the door will be a wooden beam with Grimoire 1 on top.

    • If you are having trouble reaching the Grimoire, try opening the nearby chest to get a boost.

Tome 2

  • If entering the area from the right gate, follow the city street until you reach either a broken black rail fence or an open gate. If entering the area from the left gate, follow an alley to return to the main city street and find the black rail fence.

  • Facing the direction you entered, look for a door on the far left wall. Inside will be a small chruch.

  • The second tome can be found in the chest on the center stage.

Grimoire 2

Immediately after blowing up the gate.
This Grimoire is behind a jump puzzle and may take several attempts to reach. It is not recommended to get if it seperates you from your team.

  • Once you enter the gate, follow the wall on your left until you come to a dead end with a large amount of debris.

  • Using a crate found in the rubble, jump onto a stack of two crates with a green tarp on top.

  • Turn right and use the wooden beam to jump onto the overhang.

  • Around the corner will be another overhang to jump to. Once on top, walk to the edge and look down, the grimoire will be on the ledge below.

Grimoire 3

  • While proceeding throught the inn, you will eventually have to jump over a broken door. Once over door, continue to climb stairs until you reach the rooftop.

  • Backtracking down the last staircase, you will find an alcove behind it, with a set of candles in a small recess to the right.

  • Hit these candles with a melee weapon to put them out.

  • Make your way back into the inn until you come to a door directly in front of you. Inside and to the right will be a (now opened) secret passage with the final grimoire inside.
