Warhammer: The End Times - Vermintide

Grimoire and Lore Book Locations

Use the navigation links above to select an act or DLC mission to locate the Grimoires and Tomes for each level.

Cursed Dice are randomly generated each playthrough and do not have consistent locations.

For more information about the game visit the Official Site, Steam page, or Vermintide Wiki.

Tomes

Several tomes can be found each mission. Tomes replace the primary item slot for your character, where a medical kit or healing daught would normally go, and will display a message to all party members when picked up.

At the end of every level, a "tome die" will replace one of the standard dice for each tome carried by a player. This die has a higher probability of giving an item upgrade than the white die.

There are no negative effects to carrying a tome besides taking up inventory space.

Grimoires

Grimoires replace the potion slot of your inventory, where a potion of strength or speed would normally go.

Picking up the tome reduces the health of all party members by a third. This effect stacks with multiple Grimoires.

At the end of every level, a "grimoire die" will replace one of the standard dice for each grimoire carried by a player. This die has the highest probability of giving a weapon upgrade (100%).

Cursed Dice

A Cursed Die is a special die found within chests or on the body of special rats.

When picked up, the die will give the entire team an improved chance of a weapon upgrade.

There are no negative effects to picking up a Cursed Die (despite the name), and they do not replace any inventory slots.
