Tome 1

The Dog Pens
This Grimoire is behind a jump puzzle and may take several attempts to reach. It is not recommended to get if it separates you from your team.

  1. Look for a building with a ladder attached and a flat roof and climb up.

    • The building is located off the main road in this area.

  2. Jump onto the nearby tree, then onto the slanted roof.

    • When looking at the street, the tree and building are to your left; the same side as the ladder.

  3. Look across the street to find a scaffolding on the nearby building. After reaching the scaffold, continue to your left around the corner to find a fenced in alley. Jump down to find the first grimoire in a smashed barrel.

    • There is a ladder allowing you to climb out afterwards.

Tome 1

Thulmannplatz

  1. Upon entering Thulmanplatz, follow the right wall up a staircase onto a raised section of the sidewalk.

  2. Follow this path forward until you come to a cluster of three barrels.

  3. Use the crate to the left of the barrels to look into a small wooden outcropping with the first tome inside.

Tome 2

Merchant Quarter
There are two methods of obtaining this book. See below.

Method 1

  1. After exiting the burning Granary (large building with a spiral staircase), follow the ledge to the left.

  2. In the corner there is a crate with a yellow tarp and bucket on top.

  3. Jump onto the bucket and look to the left at the crate with another yellow tarp. The second lore book is on top.

Method 2

  1. After jumping down from the burning Granary, follow the exit into the market street and look left.

  2. There should be a ladder leaning against the building leading to a wooden walkway.

  3. Follow this walkway to the end and jump down onto the second wooden walkway.

  4. Follow this walkway to the end and jump down onto the second wooden walkway.

Grimoire 2

The Carpenter's Guild

  1. When you enter the Carpenter's Guild area, you should notice a carriage in the street on your left, as well as a group of barrels against the nearby wall.

  2. Using the barrels, jump onto the front of the carriage, then onto the roof.

  3. Next, jump onto the nearby slanted roof on your left. Go to the opposite corner (diagonal).

  4. Here you should find a wooden walkway with a pile of woods within jumping distance. Jump onto it and follow the beams to the other side to find the second grimoire.

Tome 3

Merchant Quarter Cesspool

  1. Once you leave the Carpenter's Guild, you will have to fall down into the Merchant Quarter Cesspool. Once you do, turn to your left and follow the wall.

  2. You will come to a dead end with the final tome lying on the ground.
