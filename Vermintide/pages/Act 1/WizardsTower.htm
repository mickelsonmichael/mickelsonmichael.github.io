Tome 1

Main Library

  1. When you first enter the main library, take the stairs on your right up two floors. (You should now be on the 2nd floor (3rd floor UK))

  2. Travel to your right until you come to an alcove on the left leading to a room.

  3. Inside, on a table, will be the first tome.

Tome 2

The Stairway

  1. Make your way to the top level of The Stairway until you reach the exit.

    • The exit can be found behind two empty bookshelves with red curtains draped over them. Proceed past these bookshelves.

  2. Turn to the right and find the passageway leading to more stairs. You will have to break some boards to proceed.

  3. Follow this path until you reach an ammo container and some supplies. The second tome will be among them.

Grimoire 1

The Stairway
If you are still in the same area as Tome 2, return to the exit.

  1. Make your way to the top level of The Stairway until you reach the exit.

    • The exit can be found behind two empty bookshelves with red curtains draped over them. Proceed past these bookshelves.

  2. Whichever door you go through, you will reach a long hallway perpendicular to you. At one end will be a black iron gate.

  3. Turn right and travel towards the black gate until you reach an outcropping on your right.

  4. The first Grimoire will be on top of a pile of books in the corner behind the door. You must jump on the cages to reach it.

Tome 3

Hall of Illusion
Getting this Tome will require you to backtrack several rooms. It is unwise to get this Tome unless you have someone to come with you.

  1. When you enter the Hall of Illusions, you will fall through a door and land near some bookcases.

  2. Take the path left until you come to a gap in the floor.

  3. To the left of the gap will be a ledge with a sideways chest on it. Jump to the ledge and the third Tome will be inside.

    • If you miss the jump back up, you will land back in the Hall of Mysteries and will have to go back through the Hall of Knowledge.

Grimoire 2

Hall of Illusion

  1. Before you exit the Hall of Illusion look to your right and you will see a very steep staircase.

  2. You must jump up this staircase several steps at a time until you reach the door at the top. The second grimoire will be inside.
